Jim Morrison

Best remebered as the lead singer of the Doors! He is best known for his poetic lyrics, distinctive voice, wild personality, and performances.

Jim Morrison co-founded the Doors during the summer of 1965 in Venice, California. Morrison developed an alcohol dependency during the 1960s, which affected his performance on stage. He died at the age of 27 in Paris
