Who Were the Doors?

The Doors were an American rock band. They were formed in 1965 in Los Angeles. Vocalists Jim Morrison, keyboardists Ray Manzarek, guitarist Robby Krieger, and drummer John Densmore.

Psychedelic rock, blues rock, acid rock are the different types of genres. The years they are active is 1965-1973
