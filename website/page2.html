Raymond Manzarek

He co-founded the Doors from 1965 to 1973. He was notable for performing on a keyboard bass, taking the role usually filed by a bass guitar player.

Manzarek was born and raised on the South side of chicago. He was born to Helana and Raymond, a polish descendent. He married a UCLA amluma in Los Angeles.
